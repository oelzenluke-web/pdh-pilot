Privacy Policy

Version: 2026-06-10 (draft)

1. Overview

PDH Pilot ("the App") is designed to keep your data on your own device. We do not operate user accounts and we do not maintain a database of your license profile or logged hours.

2. What's Stored Locally

Your profile (license type, state, renewal date), logged PDH entries, and unlock status are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers and is removed if you clear your browser's site data.

3. AI Certificate Scanning

If you use the certificate-scanning feature, the image you upload is sent to a third-party AI provider (Anthropic) solely to extract course details (title, provider, date, hours, category). The image and extracted text are processed to generate a response and are not stored by us. Please review the certificate before uploading to ensure it doesn't contain information you don't wish to share.

4. Payments

One-time purchases are processed by Stripe. We do not see or store your card number. Stripe may collect billing information (name, email, card details, and, where applicable, tax-related location information) to process the transaction and calculate any applicable sales tax. At checkout, Stripe also records your acceptance of these Terms (timestamp and version) as part of the transaction record. See Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how Stripe handles your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The App itself does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. Stripe's checkout pages may set their own cookies as part of payment processing.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data lives in your browser's local storage, you control it directly — clearing your browser's site data for this site permanently deletes your profile, entries, and unlock status. We have no copy to delete on our end.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for licensed professionals and is not directed to children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to the contact address listed on the PDH Pilot website.

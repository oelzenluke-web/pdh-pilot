Terms of Service

Version: 2026-06-10 (draft)

1. The Service

PDH Pilot ("the App") is a tool that helps licensed Professional Engineers (PE) and Professional Land Surveyors (PLS) track Professional Development Hours (PDH) against their state board's continuing education requirements. The App is provided "as is" for informational and organizational purposes only.

2. Not Professional or Regulatory Advice

Requirement templates (hours required, ethics requirements, cycle lengths, renewal dates) are starting points based on commonly published state board rules and may be inaccurate, outdated, or incomplete. You are solely responsible for verifying your continuing education requirements directly with your state licensing board and for the accuracy of any records you submit to that board. The App does not guarantee compliance with any licensing requirement.

3. Pricing — One-Time Purchase Per Renewal Cycle

The App offers a free tier (up to 3 manually-logged entries) and a one-time paid unlock of $49 USD that covers AI certificate scanning, unlimited hour logging, audit-ready CSV export, and deadline tracking for a single renewal cycle, as defined by the renewal date in your profile. This is not a subscription — no recurring charges are made. When your renewal date changes to a new cycle, a new one-time purchase is required to unlock that cycle's features.

Where applicable, sales tax is calculated and added to the purchase price at checkout via Stripe Tax.

4. Refunds

If your state licensing board rejects your continuing education log produced using the App, you may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Refund requests should include documentation of the board's rejection.

5. AI Certificate Scanning

The certificate-scanning feature sends an image you upload to a third-party AI service for the sole purpose of extracting course title, provider, date, hours, and category. Extracted data may be inaccurate and you are responsible for reviewing and correcting it before relying on it.

6. Data Storage

Your profile, logged hours, and unlock status are stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). The App does not maintain a server-side account or database of your records. See the Privacy Policy for details.
